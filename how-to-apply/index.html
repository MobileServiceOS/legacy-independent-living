How to Apply

Getting started is simple

No long forms or red tape. Applying to Legacy is a conversation — we want to understand your situation and help you find the right next step.

Step 01

Reach out

Call or text (832) 317-1933, or send the inquiry form. Tell us a little about your situation — there's no judgment here.

Step 02

Talk it through

We'll have a short, honest conversation about your needs, our house guidelines, and whether Legacy is a good fit for where you are right now.

Step 03

Visit the home

Come see the residence in person, meet us, and picture your fresh start. We'll review expectations and answer every question.

Step 04

Move in & move forward

Once you're approved, we help you settle in and connect to the work, recovery, and community resources that keep your progress going.

Start your inquiry Call (832) 317-1933

Who we welcome

  • Adults returning to the community after incarceration (reentry)
  • People completing a treatment or recovery program
  • Anyone needing a stable, sober transitional home
  • Individuals working or actively seeking employment
  • Those ready to follow house guidelines and live respectfully
  • People rebuilding independence at their own pace
  • Residents who value peer support and accountability
  • Anyone in the Fulshear & Fort Bend County area seeking a fresh start
