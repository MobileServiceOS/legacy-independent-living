How to Apply

Moving in is simple

No long forms or red tape. Finding a room with Legacy is a quick conversation. We'll tell you what's available across Houston and help you move in.

The Process

Four simple steps

Step 01

Reach out

Call or text (832) 317-1933, or send the inquiry form. Tell us roughly where in the Houston area you want to live. There's no judgment here.

Step 02

See what's open

We'll tell you which homes have rooms available near you, what the rent is, and what to expect. Quick, honest answers, no long applications.

Step 03

Tour a room

Take a look at an available room and home so you can picture yourself there. Ask anything you want.

Step 04

Move in

When you're ready, you move in and the room is yours. From there you live independently: your schedule, your space, your life.
